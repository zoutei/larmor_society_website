EXPAND YOUR INTERSTSAcademic talks

The Larmor Society hosts a variety of talks each term spanning all areas of scientific research, which all students and other members of the university are welcome to attend free of charge. Every talk has refreshments (including wine) provided free of charge. 

Delicious FOOD, Good DRINKSSOCIAL EVENTS

We host social events during term (usually in-person, but unfortunately, remote this year) in order for different year groups to get to know each other. Apart from the good company, socials include tons of pizzas and nice drinks.

End of Academic yearGarden Party

At the end of every academic year, the Larmor Society hosts an Annual Garden Party with the Adams Society
 (Mathematics) and Parsons Society (Engineering), featuring a full hog roast. There is no better way than the Garden Party to celebrate the end of exams (apart from the May Ball, of course).

COMBINATION FORMALANNUAL DINNER

The Annual Dinner in Lent Term is hosted in the Combination Room where the D-Day invasion was planned. There is no finer place in Cambridge to have a five-course meal paired with high-quality wine.
